Baseline Type

The warlock baseline starts with 16 CHA, increases it to 18 at 4th level and 20 at 8th level, and attacks using Eldritch Blast with Agonizing Blast and Hex.

The fighter baseline starts with 16 DEX, the Archery Fighting Style, and the Crossbow Expert feat. It takes the Sharpshooter feat at 4th level and increases DEX to 18 at 6th level and 20 at 8th level.

The rogue baseline starts with 16 DEX and uses a rapier with Sneak Attack. It increases DEX to 18 at 4th level and 20 at 8th level.
